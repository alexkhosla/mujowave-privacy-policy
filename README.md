# Privacy Policy for Mujowave

  Effective date: July 5, 2026

  Mujowave is an Android sailing instrument app. It uses your phone's GPS, Bluetooth, sensors, and battery
  status to display sailing information such as speed, heading, heel, trim, GPS position, and battery
  runtime.

  ## Information Mujowave Accesses

  Mujowave may access:

  - Location data, including GPS position and speed over ground
  - Bluetooth data when connecting to compatible Mujowave hardware
  - Device sensor data, including accelerometer, gyroscope, compass, and barometer readings where
  available
  - Battery status information

  ## How Information Is Used

  This information is used only to provide Mujowave's sailing instrument features on your device.

  Mujowave does not create user accounts, does not include advertising, and does not sell personal data.

  ## Data Sharing

  Mujowave does not transmit your GPS, Bluetooth, sensor, or battery data to the developer or to a cloud
  service.

  If you connect to external Mujowave hardware over Bluetooth, data may be exchanged locally between your
  phone and that hardware to provide app functionality.

  ## Data Retention

  Mujowave does not store long-term sailing history on a server. Live instrument data is used for the
  current app session.

  ## Permissions

  Mujowave may request location and Bluetooth permissions so it can provide GPS-based sailing instruments
  and connect to compatible hardware.
